About Us

NRI Ltd is a company that provides expert advice and guidance to organizations and individuals on research, data, technology, finance, and investment-related matters. Our specific objective include the following. Conducting research and analysis: NRI conducts research and analysis on various business, social, economic, health care, investments, and financial topics, including Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) studies, market trends, customer behavior, and competitive landscapes. This research is used to inform its consulting services and provide clients with insights about their business or projects. Providing training and education: This includes offering workshops and seminars on research methods and techniques, topical issues and trends in technology and markets, as well as providing one-on-one coaching and mentorship to businesses and individuals.
